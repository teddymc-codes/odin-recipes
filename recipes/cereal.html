Cereal

Description

A delicious bowl of cereal with milk to start your day.

Ingredients

  • Cereal
  • Milk
    • Almond Milk
    • Soy Milk
    • Oat Milk
    • Cashew Milk

Steps

  1. Fill approximately 75% of a bowl with your choice of cereal.
  2. Pour milk over cereal until the level of milk matches the hight of the cereal.
  3. Enjoy!
